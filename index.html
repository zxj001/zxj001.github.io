1. Medical Center

A 2 player, split-screen 3D platformer

Play using Unity3D Web Player

(Works best with Mozilla Firefox)

Instructions:

Player 1 (left side, doctor)

  • Press Mouse 1 button to shoot a ball.
  • Use the balls to move the cells around and help your partner!

Player 2 (right side, pill)

  • Press "Left-Shift" to explode.
  • Press arrow keys to move.
    • Press "Space" to jump.
  • Press "R" to respawn.
  • DON'T TOUCH THE GROUND!
  • Navigate the level by jumping on the red-blood cells!
  • Kill the green bacteria by exploding near them!
  • Careful not to explode too close to healthy Red cells, they may get hurt!

2. High School Philosopher

Arm chair time

Play using Unity3D Web Player

(Works best with Mozilla Firefox)

Instructions:

  • Click and drag the "middle mouse button" to move around.
  • Press "SPACE" to encourage reproduction.
  • Use the buttons at the top left to give your citizens resources.
  • Click and drag a citizen to move it.
  • Get to 200 population with over 95 happiness to win!
  • Don't let happiness fall below zero!
  • Don't let all your citizens die!
