1. Medical Center

A 2 player, cooperative split-screen 3D platformer

Play using Unity3D Web Player

(Works best with Mozilla Firefox)

Instructions:

Player 1 (left side, doctor)

  • Press "Left Mouse Button" to shoot a ball.
  • Use the balls to move the cells around and help your partner!

Player 2 (right side, pill)

  • Press "Left-Shift" to explode.
  • Press arrow keys to move.
    • Press "Space" to jump.
  • Press "R" to respawn.
  • DON'T TOUCH THE GROUND!
  • Navigate the level by jumping on the red-blood cells!
  • Kill the green bacteria by exploding near them!
  • Careful not to explode too close to healthy Red cells, they may get hurt!

Notes and Tips:

  • The LOWER the score, the better
  • Respawning and killing red blood cells increases the score (bad)
  • Launching blue balls increases the score (bad)
  • Every second that passes increases the score

2. High School Philosopher

Arm chair time

Play using Unity3D Web Player

(Works best with Mozilla Firefox)

Instructions:

  • Click and drag the "Middle Mouse Button" to move around.
  • Press "Space" to encourage reproduction.
  • Use the buttons at the top left to give your citizens resources.
  • Click and drag a citizen to move it.
  • Get to 200 population with over 95 happiness to win!
  • Don't let happiness fall below zero!
  • Don't let all your citizens die!

Notes and Tips:

  • The creatures get darker (more blue) as they age.
  • The creatures flash red when angry.
    • The creatures flash green when sick.
  • The creatures reproduce with whomever their heads are pointing at.
  • When the creatures are larva (light blue) they aren't as productive.
  • When the creatures are old (grey-black) they aren't as productive.
  • Creatures that touch a green skulls (poison) take damage.
  • Creatures that die from sickness spawn green skulls (poison).
  • A Creature's productivity is loosely tied to its visual complexity.
